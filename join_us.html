We are looking for Software Engineers and Machine Learning Engineers.

You will be a good fit if you

  • Have experience training and deploying large models
  • Are comfortable with spending time understanding raw data
  • Love competing on Kaggle and obsessing with the accuracy of your models
  • Have experience of working with LLMs

Share your resume/github/kaggle at careers@agentlabs.ai if you loved our mission !

Hiring process

  • Exploratory - (30 mins phone/hangout): Learn more about our lab and discuss your career interests and past experience.
  • Technical screening - (30 mins phone/hangout): A check on basic programming skills and machine learning.
  • Technical Interview - (4 hours in the office): We expect our engineers to be ICs and independently solve hard problems. We don't ask traditional algorithm or data structure problems. You will be given a real world problem and asked to build a solution in 4 hours. You can use google, ChatGPT or any tools of your choice. We are interested in your thought process and how you attack the problem.
  • Team chat - (30 mins in the office): A chat with your potential team
  • Offer (in your email): We prefer making an offer on the same day :). No waiting for weeks !!
