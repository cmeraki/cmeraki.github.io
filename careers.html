Our mission is to ensure repetitive tasks get automated for everyone. To pursue this we are building a platform where companies outsource their repetitive tasks. We want to digitise all human skills and build AI capable of doing them !!

We are looking for Software and Machine Learning Engineers.

You may be a good fit if you:

  • Have significant software or machine learning experience
  • Have experience training and deploying large models
  • Love competing on kaggle and obsess with accuracy of your models
  • Have experience automating tasks with LLMs
  • Love solving problems with data

Share your resume/github/kaggle at careers@mgik.ai if you loved our mission !

Hiring process

  • Exploratory - (30 mins phone/hangout): Learn more about mgik and discuss your career interests and past experience
  • Technical screening - (30 mins phone/hangout): A check on basic programming skills and machine learning.
  • Technical Interview - (4 hours in mgik office): We expect our engineers to be ICs and independently solve hard problems. We don't ask traditional algorithm or data structure problems. You will be given a real world problem and asked to build a solution in 4 hours. You can use google, chat gpt or any tools of your choice. We are interested in your thought process and how you attack the problem.
  • Team chat - (30 mins in mgik office): A chat with your potential team at mgik
  • Offer (in your email): We prefer making an offer on the same day :). No waiting for weeks !!
